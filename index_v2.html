Welcome to the Velvet Side Speakeasy

Here we do all of our tastings in a blind flight of 3 or 4 bottles to ensure there is no influence or bias to our tasting notes.

Couple of things to note about our tastings. We have three sensory categories to judge each whiskey by; nose, palate, and finish on a scale of 1 to 10. Our final score for the bottle is weighted 30% of the mose score, 50% of the palate score and 20% of the finish score.

We also category our bottles by proof; flyweight (80.0 - 89.9 proof), lightweight (90.0 - 99.9 proof), middleweight (100.0 - 101.9 proof), cruiserweight (102.0 - 110.9 proof) and heavyweight (111.0+ proof)

The compass in the top right corner is a navigation tool to help you find a bottle that you think you may like; between the 3 sensory categories and 5 weight classes, there are 15 navigation buttons to select from.

We hope you find a bottle you like!
