Welcome to the Velvet Side Speakeasy

Our tastings are all performed in a blind flight of 3 or 4 bottles to ensure there is no influence or any existing bias regarding the labels on the bottle.

Couple of things to know about our tastings. We have three (3) sensory categories to evaluate each bottle; nose, palate, and finish and we score each sensory on a scale of 1 to 10. Our final score for the bottle is weighted 30% for the nose score, 50% for the palate score and 20% for the finish score.

We have also categorzied our bottles by proof; flyweight (80.0 - 89.9 proof), lightweight (90.0 - 99.9 proof), middleweight (100.0 - 101.9 proof), cruiserweight (102.0 - 110.9 proof) and heavyweight (111.0+ proof)

We also encourage you to use the navigational compass in the top right hand corner. It contains 15 categories; an intersection of the three sensory categories and the 5 weight classes.

We hope you not only find a bottle you like, but would love to hear your feeback! Remember, enjoy your whiskey journey..and that journey is best shared with close family and friends.
